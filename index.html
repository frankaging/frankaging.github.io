Hi! I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Stanford University, co-advised by Chris Manning and Chris Potts. I received my Master's degree from Symbolic Systems Program at Stanford University. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in aerospace engineering, and worked in cloud computing.

I am interested in building interpretable and robust NLP systems.
